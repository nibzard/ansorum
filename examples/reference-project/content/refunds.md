Refund details for customers.

## Eligibility

Refunds follow the [billing policy](https://example.com/policy).

Customers can usually request a refund within 30 days of the original charge
when the service was not materially used.

## Exceptions

- Renewal charges after the refund window are normally non-refundable.
- Usage-based add-ons are reviewed case by case.
- Credits issued to an account may replace a cash refund when the billing issue
  is operational rather than contractual.

## Where to ask

Contact billing support with the invoice number and the reason for the request.
